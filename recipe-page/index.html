Cayeye Recipe

Cayeye is a traditional dish from the Caribbean region of Colombia. The main ingredients are green bananas that are boiled and pureed, and it is topped with fresh cheesse('Queso Costeno', in Colombia) or a sofrito(salsa). Cayeye is usually serve for breakfast.

Photo credit: Los Antojos De Clara

ingredients

  • 6 green bananas
  • Salt to taste
  • 2-3 Tbsp. of butter (or more to taste)
  • 1/2 lb grated fresh Mexican cheesse
  • Sour Cream to top
  • Milk

Directions

  1. In a large pot, place enough water to boil with salt to taste. While the water is boiling, peel the bananas. Once the water is boiling, add the bananas and cook them for 30 minutes until they are soft enough.
  2. Remove the bananas in a bowl, mash them with a fork, add butter, and a splahs of milk (must be warm).
  3. Add the grated cheese and mix well. Add salt to taste, and top with a sour cream and serve.
