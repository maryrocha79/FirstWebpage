Cayeye Recipe

Cayeye is a traditional dish from the Caribbean region of Colombia. The main ingredients are green bananas that are boiled and pureed, and it is topped with fresh cheesse('Queso Costeno', in Colombia) or a sofrito. Cayeye is usually serve for breakfast.

ingredients

  • 6 green bananas
  • Salt to taste
  • 2-3 Tbsp. of butter (or more to taste)
  • 1/2 lb Fresh Mexican cheesse
  • Sour Cream to top
  • Milk
